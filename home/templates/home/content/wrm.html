Water Resource Management (WRM)

Water Resource Management (WRM) ensures sustainable water supply, efficient demand forecasting, and equitable resource allocation through data-driven approaches.

Major Submodules and Outcomes

Demand and Forecasting

Accurate demand assessment ensures water availability while preventing overuse and scarcity. This module provides real-time monitoring and predictive analytics.

  • IoT-enabled smart meters, SCADA systems, and satellite data for real-time monitoring.
  • Machine learning models predict water demand based on demographic and consumption trends.
  • Prevents overloading of distribution systems and reduces non-revenue water (NRW).

Resource Allocation

Ensures equitable, sustainable, and resilient water supply under changing environmental conditions.

  • Evaluates long-term sustainability of water sources (rivers, lakes, groundwater).
  • GIS-based mapping for optimal resource allocation.
  • Supports inter-basin water transfers and integrated supply planning.
