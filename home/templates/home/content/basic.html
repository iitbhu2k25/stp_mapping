Varuna River & SLCR

Varuna River and SLCR (Sustainable Land and Water Resource Conservation) are critical for water management and conservation in regions dependent on river systems. The Varuna River faces challenges related to water quality, availability, and seasonal flow, which require coordinated management interventions.

Outputs include reports on river health, rejuvenation strategies, water quality improvement plans, and SLCR implementation documents, as well as data-driven decision tools for better management.
